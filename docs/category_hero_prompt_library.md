# Product Category Hero Illustration Prompt Library

This document serves as the permanent reference and single source of truth for the generation and maintenance of the product category hero illustrations on `peroniks.id`.

---

## Global Requirements & Standard Parameters

All image generations must share the following configuration parameters to ensure a completely cohesive printed engineering catalogue style:

* **Composition**: Centered single product or clean grouping, occupying approximately 70% of the frame.
* **Camera Angle**: 45-degree isometric or standard studio catalog perspective, identical across all categories.
* **Lighting**: Balanced, high-end studio lighting with realistic soft diffuse reflections and soft natural contact shadows on the ground.
* **Background**: Seamless pure white studio background (`#ffffff`).
* **Negative Prompt**: `text, watermark, logo, labels, blueprint, exploded view, decorative props, low quality, cartoon, illustration, drawing, unrealistic reflection, orange handle, red handle`.
* **Style**: Photorealistic premium industrial product photography.

## Category Specifications

### 1. Stainless Steel Flanges
* **Design Intent**: Showcase high-quality silver flanges with clean machined circles.
* **Representative Product Families**: Plate Flange, Blind Flange, Welding Neck Flange.
* **Visual Characteristics**: Machined bolt circles, raised faces, center bore.
* **Material Finish**: Polished stainless steel.
* **Surface Texture**: Machined lathe rings, high specular metallic highlights.
* **Camera Angle**: Catalog isometric perspective.
* **Lighting**: Bright studio lighting with sharp specular metallic highlights.
* **Background**: Pure white.
* **Final Generation Prompt**: `Premium industrial product photography of a polished stainless steel plate flange, centered composition, metallic reflections, machined finish, silver color, isometric catalog perspective, pure white studio background, soft natural contact shadow.`
